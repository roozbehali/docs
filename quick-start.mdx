## Initial Setup

Head to the [signup page](https://usebutler.app/signup) and make an account -
we offer passwordless auth and Google OAuth. Then, you'll be taken to our onboarding flow.

<Steps>
	<Step title="Invite code">
		If you're the first user in your organization, you'll be prompted to enter
		the invite code you received from us. 
		
		If your team lead has already created
		an organization, enter the code that you received from them.
		<Frame>
			<img src="/images/invite_code.png" />
		</Frame>
		<Warning>
			Butler is in private beta and invite codes are required. If you would like
			to try Butler, please reach out to us at
			[contact@usebutler.app](mailto:contact@usebutler.app).
		</Warning>
	</Step>
	<Step title="Details">
		Fill out your personal (and organization-specific, if needed) details.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Team-wide invite code">
    	If you're the first user in your organization, send the generated code to your team.
		<Frame>
			<img src="/images/team_invite.png" />
		</Frame>
    </Step>

</Steps>
